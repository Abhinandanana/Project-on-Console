How to set up Visual Studios?

1. First Install the Visual Studios

2. Then after opening the platform search on the search bar "Live Server"

3. Click on the first option you get after searching which will be "Live Server"

4. After clicking on "Live Server" there will be an option to Install it. Click on Install.

5. Then make a folder on your Desktop named- "Visual Studios"

6. Then make another folder in it of the name of your choice for eg- Class77.

7. Go back to Visual Studios and click on "File."p>

8. Then Click on "Open Folder" and select the folder which you named.

9. Right Click on the Greyish Bar on your screen and click "New File".

10. Name your File "index.html"

11. Start Coding!
